Research Projects

Our research focuses on Swarm Intelligence and its applications in multi-robot systems.


PINN + Safe MARL New Direction

Physics-Informed Safe Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning for Swarm Robots

物理信息驱动的安全多智能体强化学习 [Detailed Notes]


Research Goal: Develop safe and efficient multi-robot control algorithms that incorporate physics priors and safety guarantees.


Key Technologies:

  • Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINN) - Encode robot dynamics and physical constraints
  • Control Barrier Functions (CBF) - Real-time safety guarantees
  • Hamilton-Jacobi Reachability - Safety-critical neighbor selection
  • Decentralized MARL - Scalable multi-agent training
