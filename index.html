Research Directions

Our research explores Swarm Intelligence principles and their applications in multi-robot systems. [Full Details]

  • Multi-Agent Cooperative Localization
    Distributed localization using reinforcement learning, federated learning, and belief propagation for robot teams.
  • Sim2Real Transfer
    Bridging the reality gap between simulation and real-world deployment using domain randomization, GNN-based transfer, and Real2Sim2Real methods.
  • Explainable Swarm Intelligence (xSwarm)
    Making swarm behavior transparent and interpretable through LLM-powered agents, temporal logic explanations, and audit trails.
  • Safe & Secure Swarm Robotics
    Defense against adversarial attacks (data poisoning, Byzantine attacks) using blockchain consensus and quarantine strategies.
  • Human Activity Recognition
    Deep learning and WiFi CSI-based sensing for activity recognition (InnoHAR, WiDriver).
  • Near-Ground Localization & UWB
    UWB radio channel modeling and IMU/TOA fusion for accurate ground robot positioning.
